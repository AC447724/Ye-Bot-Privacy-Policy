Ye Bot - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 13, 2025

At Ye Bot, we understand that your privacy is paramount. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Discord bot. By inviting Ye Bot to your server or interacting with its features, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Ye Bot is designed with privacy in mind, collecting only the essential data required for its operation and to provide you with a seamless experience. We collect the following types of information:

  • Discord Server IDs: To identify the servers Ye Bot is a part of, enabling server-specific configurations, moderation settings, and command prefixes.
  • Discord User IDs: To manage user-specific features such as command cooldowns, personalized settings (if implemented in the future), and to track bot usage for statistical purposes.
  • Command Usage Data: Anonymous data on which commands are used, how frequently, and general interaction patterns. This helps us understand bot performance and identify areas for improvement. This data does NOT include message content or personal conversations.

We do NOT collect:

  • Your personal messages, private conversations, or any content from your Discord interactions.
  • Any personally identifiable information beyond your Discord User ID and Server ID.
  • Any sensitive personal data such as your real name, email address, IP address, or location.

2. How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes, ensuring Ye Bot functions effectively and continues to evolve:

  • To Operate and Maintain Ye Bot: Server IDs are used to apply server-specific settings and moderation rules. User IDs are used for command processing and cooldowns.
  • To Improve Ye Bot's Features: Anonymous command usage data helps us understand popular features, identify bugs, and plan future updates to enhance your experience.
  • To Ensure Compliance: Data may be used to enforce our Terms of Service and Discord's Community Guidelines, preventing misuse or abuse of the bot.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your trust is invaluable. Ye Bot does not share, sell, rent, or trade your collected information with any third parties under any circumstances. All data remains within the bot's operational infrastructure and is accessible only to authorized developers for maintenance and improvement purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain collected data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. Server IDs and User IDs are retained while Ye Bot is active in a server or while a user is actively interacting with the bot. Anonymous command usage data may be retained for longer periods for historical analysis and trend identification.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to understand and control your data. As a user of Ye Bot, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request information about the data we hold related to your interactions with Ye Bot.
  • Right to Rectification: If any data we hold is inaccurate, you can request its correction.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request the deletion of your data from our systems. Note that this may impact Ye Bot's functionality for you.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your data for certain purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the methods provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

6. Security of Your Information

We implement robust security measures to protect the integrity and confidentiality of your data. This includes secure server environments, access controls, and encryption where appropriate. While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and, if feasible, through an announcement in the official Ye Bot support server. Your continued use of Ye Bot after any changes signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us: Reach Out to the Visionaries

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or if you wish to exercise your data rights, please reach out to us through the official Ye Bot support server or contact the bot's developer directly via Discord.
